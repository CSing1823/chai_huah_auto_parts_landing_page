About Chai Huah Auto Parts & Service

Established in 1997 with a passion for automotive excellence, Chai Huah Auto Parts & Service has been a trusted name in Banting for providing top-notch auto repair and maintenance services. Our team of certified technicians brings years of expertise to every job. We also serve as a reliable spare parts retailer for the automotive community.

We specialize in general auto repairs, maintenance, diagnostics, and spare parts supply. Whether it's routine maintenance or complex repairs, we ensure your vehicle is in the best hands.
